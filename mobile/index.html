Click the LAYERS Menu in the top right to show or hide the layer menu, then click on a map layer in the list to make it appear on the globe. Click the layer again to remove it from the globe. Click the folder icon next to any layer to show layer details and additional layer controls. For more information, Watch the Video on YouTube.
